Machine learning is a subfield of artificial intelligence (AI). The goal of machine learning generally is to understand the structure of data and fit that data into models that can be understood and utilized by people.

This is a project that implements Artificial Intelligence to classify 5 sports celebrities namely Lionel messi, Maria Sharapova, Roger Frederer, Virat Kohli, Serena Williams.This project includes working with and cleaning large amounts of data, learning from scratch and implementing state of the art techniques, resorting to innovative thinking to solve challenges. To achieve this task, we developed a Machine Learning model that yielded an average accuracy of 84% over the caterogies.
